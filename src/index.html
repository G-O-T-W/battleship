Battleship

Welcome to Battleship

Before you proceed with the game, please review the rules:

  • The game is played on a 10×10 grid.
  • Each player places 5 ships on their grid:
    • Carrier – 5 cells
    • Battleship – 4 cells
    • Cruiser – 3 cells
    • Submarine – 3 cells
    • Destroyer – 2 cells
  • Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically.
  • Ships cannot overlap or go outside the grid.
  • Players take turns attacking one cell.
  • A hit damages a ship. A miss ends the turn.
  • A ship is sunk when all of its cells are hit.
  • The first player to sink all 5 enemy ships wins.
